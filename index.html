What is Bike Buddies?

Bike Buddies is a non-profit organization with the intent to unite cyclists around the city. Bike Buddies wants to bring individuals together with common interests and goals.

Why Bike Buddies?

Simply put: we love to ride and we want to ride with other people who love to ride.
